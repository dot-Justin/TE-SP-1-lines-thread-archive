<p>additionally, i’d like to write the glitch trigger code on the beaglebone’s pru so that the timing/delay is done in assembly rather than the <code>__delay_cycles()</code> method in C to ensure high precision/predictability. also we need to find an appropriate voltage for the glitch “command”–limitedresults doesn’t show it on their oscilloscope screenshots but the measurement at the bottom of their screen is 5V.</p>
