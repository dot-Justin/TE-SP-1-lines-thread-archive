<p>On the topic of the original use case of the SP: Does anyone find the MIDI out to be usable? I’ve tested it fairly extensively, and it doesn’t appear that it sends a Start/Stop command, just clock. So it’s fairly hard to sync up, especially considering the audio has to “wind up” for a ms or so. It’s almost like you have to try and press play on another device at the perfect time to get usable sync.</p>
