<p>Hi all, I’m back with a “small” update.</p>
<p>The discord (that may or may not exist) woke up again recently and worked their magic… they cracked the TE bootloader and created an online update utility that can update the firmware of the stem player without ever opening up the device!</p>
<p>This means that all I wrote here about needing to open up the device to first load a bootloader is no longer true. I’ll also have to change my firmware a bit because I now have to play by the rules of the TE bootloader, but that’s not going to be a problem.</p>
