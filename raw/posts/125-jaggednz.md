<p>I can provide some advice on executing the hardware modifications as I’ve managed similar scale work in the past. Minimum equipment would be: temp controlled iron with a fine tip (pinecil, ts100 or similar), fine tip metal tweezers, 0.6mm rosin core solder, flux, kapton tape, “magnet” wire or very fine gauge Teflon/ptfe wire,  a digital microscope, steady hands and a lot of patience!</p>
