<p>I haven’t really tried yet, but we’ve fully decoded the audio and LED data format, as you can see in my demo. (Again, many thanks to <a class="mention" href="/u/josejx">@JoseJX</a> for his work and advice!!)</p>
<p>The audio data format is a bit overly complicated for my taste, with very strange byte interleaving AND sample interleaving. Probably to obfuscate the original audio from Ye and stop us from ripping the original stems. We did it anyway.</p>
<p>I don’t foresee any problems loading my own stems (except speed). This is going to be my next challenge. I haven’t decided yet if I’ll change the data format or write software to convert PCM files to the weird stem player format. There is still plenty of space (2GB) on the flash memory to mess around with other stems.</p>
